When floods rise, communication should not drown.

Flood Safe is a nonprofit dedicated to rapid disaster communication. Our mission is simple: give flood victims a one-tap way to call for help, connect NGOs on one network, and deliver donations where they matter most.

With our app, every SOS signal is heard, every response is coordinated, and every donation is transparent.

Our Mission

Flood Safe exists to close the communication gap in disasters. During floods, phone lines collapse, power cuts silence communities, and victims are left unseen. We build tools that keep signals alive and responders connected.

  • 74+ communities have tested our prototype.
  • 12 NGO partners using our command dashboard.
  • Average SOS delivery time: under 5 seconds.
  • 100% transparency in donation flows.

The Flood Safe App

Built for the toughest conditions, our app works online or offline, sending SOS alerts through cellular, Wi-Fi, or mesh networks. NGOs can coordinate seamlessly and volunteers also have the oppotunity to take action.

One-Tap SOS

Immediate distress alert with GPS, battery level, and hazard tags.

Mesh Networking

Even if towers fail, phones connect peer-to-peer until help is reached.

NGO Command Rooms

Verified responders share maps, tasks, and updates in one secure space.

Data Privacy

Minimal data stored, encrypted communication by default.

Policy & Advocacy

Technology alone is not enough. That’s why Flood Safe advocates for emergency communication policies worldwide:

  • Temporary roaming rights across all carriers in disaster zones.
  • Emergency spectrum allocation for humanitarian use.
  • Priority SOS channels for life-saving communication.

Our current petition calls on the U.S. FCC to extend communication laws to humanitarian needs — because silence should never be the cost of a flood.

  • WE ARE NOT ENDORSED OR WORKING WITH THE FCC! However, as part of our advocacy mission, we are urging the FCC to extend U.S communication laws for humanitarian channels.
